# Event Report: “Event GameDay - Battle of 8 Teams”

### Event Information

- **Time:** 09:00, June 20, 2026
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

### Key Highlights

The event took place in a **live Q&A GameDay** format, where the top 8 selected teams competed against each other regarding their knowledge of the AWS ecosystem.
- **Competition format:** In each match, 2 teams faced off directly by selecting **question sets**.
- **Question set structure:** Each set consisted of **10 multiple-choice questions** or situational problems related to AWS.
- **Increasing difficulty:** The distribution of easy and difficult questions depended on the current round. The deeper the teams progressed, the higher the proportion of difficult and complex questions appeared.

### Event Experience

Watching the intense competition among the 8 teams live was an incredibly exciting experience:
- **Feeling the thrill with the teams:** The competitive atmosphere was highly dramatic. Following their strategies for choosing question sets and how the two teams chased each other closely in scores while answering AWS questions brought me moments of intense focus.
- **Absorbing knowledge naturally:** By listening to the content of the questions and viewing the explanatory answers from the organizers, I felt like I was taking a practical AWS knowledge test, which helped me review and memorize information quickly.
- **Learning from speed of thought:** Seeing how contestants quickly analyzed keywords in questions and eliminated inappropriate AWS services in a short amount of time was an amazing skill to witness.

#### Lessons Learned
- **Consolidating foundational AWS knowledge:** Realizing that the amount of questions went deep into the nuances of each service (EC2, S3, IAM, VPC, etc.), I recognized the importance of clearly understanding every small feature and best practice to avoid confusion among services with similar functions.
- **Practicing judgment and quick response skills:** When facing difficult questions in deeper rounds, sharpness and the process of elimination are key. This made me aware of the necessity to regularly take practice tests to get accustomed to time pressure.
- **Learning motivation:** Watching the seniors and contestants perform excellently gave me tremendous motivation to continue studying, obtain more AWS certifications, and hope for the opportunity to become a contestant in future GameDays.
