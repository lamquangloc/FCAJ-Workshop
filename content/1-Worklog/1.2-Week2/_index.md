### Week 2 Objectives:

* Learn the fundamentals of Amazon EC2, including instance types, AMI, storage, security, networking, monitoring, and management.
* Learn the fundamentals of Amazon S3, including buckets, objects, access control, versioning, replication, and storage features.
* Learn the fundamentals of Amazon RDS, including DB instances, subnet groups, encryption, Multi-AZ, read replicas, snapshots, and monitoring.

* Understood Amazon EC2 fundamentals.
	* Learned instance types, AMI, EBS storage, security groups, key pairs, networking, monitoring, and management.
